Summertime Sadness T-Shirt Design

Objective

Create a t-shirt design with Illustrator using spot colours, knockouts and screen plates.

Concept

The concept behind this t-shirt design was to create a design that was targetted at the 16-24 age demographic. The design is colourful and simplistic. It is cutesty, as well as a bit grunge-style due to the irony of the content.

This was created in the month of September and was inspired by the cold weather approaching and summer melting away.
