Izze Ad Campaign

04/22/2014

Objective

Create a promotional campaign that foccusses on the natural ingredients in Izze. The campaign must contain three seperate images that relate to one another and must be used in a print application, outdoor application and web application.

Concept

The concept of this campaign was to target the secondary target audience, create a compelling design and focus on Izze's natural goodness. In order to target the 20 year old age demographic, I chose to create a highly Photoshopped and captivating design. The explosive and powerful design is complemented by the bold typography and contrasted with the white space for added focus on the design.
