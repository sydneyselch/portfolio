Ikea App

Objective

TO create an app that had not been yet created that will relieve pain points in daily life.

Concept

The conept behind this app was to create an app that would aleviate common pain points when assembling ikea furniture. The happ has multiple user experience features that will improve the assembly process. On the start screen you have 3 options of how to view your product. The easiest method is to scan the barcode on the side of the box. Another option is to search the product within the categories and catalogue. ONce you have found your product you now have all of the needed information to assemble it effectively. On the start screen fo your product you have multiple views to help you. It shows a photograph of the final prouct as a diagram or sketch is sometimes unclear. Form there you can see the steps in a direction view and you can toggle down to the menu for more details and a link to a video displaying the step.If you dont want to follow along with the directions. you can watch a otion grpahics vdeo showing you step by step how yo assemble
