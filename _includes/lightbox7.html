Blood Diamond Movie Poster

Objective

Create a movie poster that contains spot colours and varnishes.

Concept

The concept behind this design was to create a more abstract poster design that resembles and symbolizes the feelings of the movie. I used scans from line art drawings in an old book, and paired them with blood splatters that drip into the shape of the map of Africa.

This would be a great concept and design for the 10 year anniversary dvd that would be released in 2016.
